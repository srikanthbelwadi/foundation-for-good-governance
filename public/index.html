Our Mission

Championing Ethical, Open & Accountable Governance

Founded in California in 2025 and recognized by the IRS as a public charity, Foundation for Good Governance USA Inc. is dedicated to creating lasting positive change in communities around the world.

"To foster and facilitate enhanced citizen involvement and engagement on a day-to-day basis in charitable activities related to poverty alleviation, educational advancement, health improvement and government efficiency globally."

What We Do

Our Core Programs & Activities

Through direct programming, advocacy, and resource distribution, we address the root causes of social inequity and governance failures across communities worldwide.

🗳️

Civic Education & Engagement

Raising public awareness and empowering citizens to participate actively in democratic processes, local governance, and civic life on a day-to-day basis.

📋

Public Policy Research

Conducting evidence-based research and publishing policy recommendations to promote transparency, accountability, and efficient governance practices at all levels.

🤝

Poverty Alleviation

Addressing and alleviating the root causes and impacts of poverty through direct programming, partnerships, and targeted resource distribution in underserved communities.

📚

Educational Advancement

Improving access to and quality of education globally — from early childhood programs to professional development initiatives — bridging the opportunity gap for all learners.

🏥

Health Improvement

Enhancing global health outcomes through direct health programming, advocacy for equitable health access, and strategic distribution of health resources.

🌐

Government Efficiency

Supporting and collaborating with organizations worldwide that share our mission to build more efficient, transparent, and responsive government institutions.

Tax-Exempt Status

Your Donation is 100% Tax-Deductible

Foundation for Good Governance USA Inc. is recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)(3) public charity. Every contribution you make may be deducted from your federal taxes.

💰

Tax Deduction for Donors

Under IRC Section 170, your donations to our organization are fully tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Always keep your official donation receipt for your records.

📜

Official IRS Approval

We received our IRS determination letter on December 15, 2025, confirming our 501(c)(3) public charity status, effective May 13, 2025 — the date of our formation.

🔍

Public Transparency

As a 509(a)(2) public charity, we are required to maintain financial transparency and file annual information returns (Form 990/990-EZ) with the IRS.

🧾

Official Donation Receipts

Every donor receives an official tax receipt stating no goods or services were exchanged for the contribution — qualifying it fully for the charitable deduction.

🌍

Global Reach

Our 501(c)(3) status allows us to fund charitable activities domestically and internationally, extending our impact to communities around the world.

⚖️

Non-Political

As required by law, we do not support or oppose political candidates or parties. Our work is strictly focused on charitable, educational, and civic purposes.
